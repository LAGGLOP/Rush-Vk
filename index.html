Belarus is a country popular for its historical architecture and buildings. The main languages ​​are Russian and Belarusian. The capital of Belarus is Minsk, located in its center. Belarus include of Minsk, Grodno, Mogilev, Vitebsk, Gomel and Brest regions. Belarus is a presidential republic. It was formed on March 25, 1918.

Geography

Belarus is located in eastern Europe and surrounded only by land. The neighboring countries of Belarus are Russia, Ukraine, Poland, Lithuania and Latvia.

Delicacies

Basically, the delicacies of Belarus are potato dishes. It are potato pancakes, dumplings, sorcerer's, potato casseroles, and etc.
